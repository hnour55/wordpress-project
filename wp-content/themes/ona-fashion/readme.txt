== Description ==

Ona Fashion is a Full Site Editing block WordPress theme based on the minimal design style and dark color scheme. It comes with various pre-defined block patterns and 8 styles variations. It's easy to customize colors and fonts via global options. This sleek, sophisticated design caters to fashion-forward brands that dare to stand out in the crowded digital landscape. When it comes to speed and performance there is no match to Ona theme, it is perfectly optimized for SEO and will boost your rankings. Perfect for designers, magazines, and fashion blogs that aren't afraid to embrace the edge of style. Make your mark in the fashion world with a website that's as cutting-edge as your designs.
✶ Demo: https://ona.deothemes.com/fashion ✶
